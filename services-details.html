Process Automation and Quality Assurance

Enterprises several workflows to ensure that their processes and procedures, compliances and regulations are adhered to while their staff perform various of their duties and responsibilities. Many of these workflows have humans in the loop and many a times this sort of human dependencies put a brake on otherwise smooth flow of operations and information.

Process automation in general addresses notion of automaton of a certain sequence of activities. In a particular context of robotic process automation, this involves activities wherein humans are also involved. Robotic Process Automation provides with an unlimited resource pool that mimics human behavior and automates routine, repetitive tasks that hamper employees’ productivity. This resource pool is not made up of human oid robots, but software code that is delivered from the cloud to any location.

Advancements in machine learning have led to technology leaders pushing for democratization of artificial intelligence, where high-level technologies are available to everyone at an affordable price. Robotics process automation technology goes a long way towards realizing that reality.

Irrespective of the industry or domain, the back-office employees are often the glue that holds together the enterprise. These workers must have the ability to multitask feverishly, be good with numbers, be a people person, be a spreadsheet expert, have a keen sense of business processes and have business intelligence, amongst many desired skills. Robotics process automation technology can be deployed right across most service departments that allow back office leaders streamline their operations so maximum efficiency can be reached through a positive synergy.

Robotic Process Automation is a technology enabler for automating business processes. It can replace interaction of people with applications with software robots that drive application user interfaces in the same way. Robotic Process Automation tools can be designed for various scenarios where repetitive tasks are involved so that the tools can interpret the tasks and decide on the optimal responses based on predefined rules.

Packaged Software

Enterprises face several tough challenges in developing and maintaining custom software applications whether it is ensuring that products meet business requirements, adopting the next generation technologies or providing the appropriate level of sustenance and support.

Software packages perform business-specific functions, as opposed to operating system or environmental software Most important is the fact that while operating or environmental software upgrades are typically transparent to the business community, application software packages address gaps in the IT processes and services. From this point of view, software packages impact the end user operations. An example, is loan processing within, say, auto dealer. A suitable loan application processing software enables the dealer to provide more effective service to their customers. So, identifying the gaps, selecting the most appropriate third-party software package and integrating the same with the existing IT infrastructure so as to derive the maximum benefits from the software package become a key exploratory requirement of any enterprise. Data integration is yet another key requirement of any enterprise.
